## 2. Players

### 2.1. Age requirements
2.1.1. Minimum age: **16 years old**.  
2.1.2. Under 18 require guardian consent.  
2.1.3. Violations lead to disqualification.

### 2.2. Accounts
2.2.1. Valid Steam account with CS2 required.  
2.2.2. FACEIT account required.  
2.2.3. FACEIT Anti-Cheat mandatory for Online Qualifiers.

### 2.3. Nicknames
Must not contain offensive, political, or inappropriate content.

### 2.4. Residency / eligibility
- Open to players residing in **Europe**  
- Must comply with Valve VRS regional rules  
- Teams must be able to attend LAN Finals in Portugal  

### 2.5. Obligations
Follow admin instructions, behave professionally, ensure stable conditions.

## 3. Team Regulations

### 3.1. Team size
- 5 core players  
- Optional: 1 substitute, 1 coach  

### 3.2. Team name & branding
Names and logos must be appropriate; changes require approval.

### 3.3. Team captain
The captain is the team’s primary contact.

### 3.4. Substitutions
Allowed only with admin approval; eliminated team members cannot be used.

### 3.5. Roster lock
Rosters lock at registration deadline.

## 4. Tournament Format

### 4.1. Online Qualifiers
- 2 Open Qualifiers  
- Platform: FACEIT  
- Format: Single Elimination or Swiss (TBA)  
- BO1 early rounds, BO3 from Quarterfinals  
- Top 2 teams from each → LAN Finals  

### 4.2. LAN Finals
- Location: **EXSAD Gaming Lounge – Barcelos, Portugal**  
- 4 teams  
- Single Elimination  
- Semifinals: BO3  
- Grand Final: BO3 or BO5 (TBA)  

### 4.3. Match schedule
Teams must be ready 15 minutes before scheduled matches.

## 5. Game Rules

### 5.1. Game version
Latest CS2 stable version unless otherwise instructed.

### 5.2. Map pool
Valve Active Duty map pool:
Ancient, Dust2, Inferno, Mirage, Nuke, Overpass, Train

### 5.3. Match settings
- MR12  
- Overtime: MR6 ($12,500)  
- Freeze time: 20s  
- Bomb timer: 40s  

### 5.4. Map veto procedure
BO1 and BO3 veto procedures follow standard Valve / Tier 2 tournament logic.

### 5.5. Knife round
Used for side selection where applicable.

### 5.6. Pauses
Tactical: 3 x 30s  
Technical: Up to 10 minutes (unless extended)

### 5.7. Seeding
1. VRS  
2. HLTV  
3. FACEIT ELO
